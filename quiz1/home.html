Surabaya Food

Most Popular Local Food

Rawon Rp 10k
Rawon is a beef stew colored with special spices, such as kluwek (a black fruit) which gives it a unique and savory taste.
Rujak Cingur Rp 33k
Rujak cingur is a mixture of vegetables, tofu, and slices of cingur (cooked beef nose dish) served with peanut sauce and petis (fermented shrimp).
Lontong Balap Rp 20k
Snacks consisting of lontong (ketupat), bean sprouts, fried tofu, lentho (tempeh mendoan), and doused with peanut sauce and special spices.
Tahu Tek-Tek Rp 10k
Fried tofu is crushed and mixed with peanut sauce, sweet soy sauce, fried onions and crackers.
Soto Ayam Rp 15k
Soto ayam Surabaya is famous for its chicken broth which is rich in spices and served with noodles, sliced chicken, boiled eggs and emping (melinjo crackers).
Semanggi Surabaya Rp 5k
Semanggi is a salad dish based on cassava leaves, mixed with bean sprouts, fried tofu, crackers, and drizzled with peanut sauce and petis.
Nasi Krawu Rp 12k
Nasi krawu is rice served with sliced beef, pindang egg and typical Surabaya chili sauce.
Tutut Rp 35k
Tutut is a seafood dish in the form of river snails cooked in spicy sauce. This dish is quite rare and unique in Surabaya.
